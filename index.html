About

Advent of machine generated speech poses serious security and privacy threats through deepfakes and speaker identity leakage. Speech Arena Team promotes open speech security research by adressing challenges in speech deepfake detection and voice privacy through different research projects - benchmarks to evaluate spoofing countermeasures and voice anonymization systems, open source toolkits for evaluation and open source models that can surpass commercial systems

Research Projects

Speech Deepfake Arena

Maintainers: Ajinkya Kulkarni, Atharva Kulkarni, Sandipana Dowerah

A regularly update leaderboard that benchmarks generalizability of speech antispoofing systems across 14 different datasets. Includes 6 commercial and 17 open source systems
