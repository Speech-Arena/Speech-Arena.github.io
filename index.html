Speech DF Arena: Benchmarking Speech DeepFake Detection Models

This website provides an overview of our research on Speech DeepFake Arena, a comprehensive benchmark for audio deepfake detection. Below you will find our research abstract, along with supplementary tables showing accuracy and F1 scores for various detection systems across multiple datasets. For the main interactive leaderboard showing EER scores, please visit Huggingface.

Abstract

Parallel to the development of advanced deepfake audio generation, audio deepfake detection has also seen significant progress. However, a standardized, and comprehensive benchmark is still missing. To address this, we introduce Speech DeepFake (DF) Arena, the first comprehensive benchmark for audio deepfake detection. Speech DF Arena provides a toolkit to uniformly evaluate the detection systems, currently across 14 diverse datasets and attack scenarios, standardized evaluation metrics and protocols for reproducibility and transparency. It also includes a leaderboard to compare and rank the systems to help researchers and developers enhance their reliability and robustness. Our study presents many systems exhibiting high EER in out-of-domain scenarios highlighting the need for benchmarking. The leaderboard is hosted on Huggingface and a toolkit for reproducing results across the listed datasets is available on GitHub.
