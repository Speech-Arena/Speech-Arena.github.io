Speech DF Arena

The advent of machine generated speech calls for dedicated research to develop countermeasure systems to protect against their misuse. The speech DF arena leaderboard provides a standardized platform to compare different speech deepfake detection approaches and ranks them on Huggingface. By assessing models across diverse datasets and attack scenarios, the speech DF arena leaderboard aims to help researchers and developers enhance the reliability and robustness of deepfake detection systems , ensuring safer and more trustworthy audio communications The main leaderboard can be found at Huggingface.
